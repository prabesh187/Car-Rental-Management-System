## **PROCESS DESCRIPTIONS**

### **Level 1 Processes:**
1. **User Management** - Handle user registration, authentication, and profile management
2. **User Authentication** - Verify user credentials and manage sessions
3. **Booking Management** - Process car rental bookings from start to finish
4. **Car Availability** - Check and manage vehicle availability in real-time
5. **Search & Recommendation** - Provide intelligent car search and personalized recommendations
6. **Driver Assignment** - Assign optimal drivers to bookings using algorithms
7. **Payment Processing** - Handle secure payment transactions and billing
8. **Booking Confirmation** - Generate confirmations and manage booking status
9. **Reports & Analytics** - Generate business intelligence and performance reports
10. **Fleet Management** - Manage client vehicles and driver assignments
11. **System Administration** - Handle system configuration and user management

### **Key Data Flows:**
- **Registration Data** - User information flowing from external entities to user management
- **Authentication Requests** - Login credentials and session management
- **Booking Requests** - Car rental requests with customer preferences
- **Availability Data** - Real-time car and driver availability information
- **Payment Information** - Secure financial transaction data
- **Confirmation Data** - Booking confirmations and receipts
- **Analytics Data** - Business intelligence and reporting information

This comprehensive DFD structure shows how your car rental management system processes data through multiple levels of detail, from high-level context to detailed sub-processes, ensuring complete coverage of all system functionality.
